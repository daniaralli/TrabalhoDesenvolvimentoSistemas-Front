CP MANAGER

ABOUT ME

CP Mananger is a management tool for teachers to control the activities of each of their students. In this platform, it is possible to insert, edit and remove the information about the work done, as well as the data of the students oriented, choose the category in which the work was carried out and other information about the orientation.

Dani Arali


Danielli Araújo Lima received a B.Sc., M.Sc. and Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at the National University of Uberlândia, Brazil, in 2010, 2012 and 2017, with their respective emphases: cryptography, artificial intelligence and robotics. She is a full-time professor at the Federal Institute of Triangulo Mineiro, Campus Patrocínio and teaches courses in the areas of discrete mathematics, procedural programming and computational intelligence. She is currently specializing in Analysis and Development of Systems Applied to Business Management Course with emphasis on Software Engineering at the Federal Institute of the Triângulo Mineiro, Campus Uberlândia. At the same time she is reviewer of International Journal of Robotic Engineering and Advances in Science, Technology and Engineering Systems Journal. Her research interest is in the theory of cellular automata and its applications including encryption and complex systems modeling. Has experience in Computer Science, focusing on Artificial Intelligence, Cryptography, and Bio-Inspired Computation, acting on the following subjects (main keywords): Cellular Automata, Stochastic Models for Interdisciplinary Problems (Pedestrian Dynamics, Biological and Physical Applications, Ants), Robots Control (Surveillance and Search and Rescue tasks, Mobile Cyber-Physical Systems, Collective Intelligence, Artificial Neural-nets), Chaos and Dynamical Systems, Graphs, Queues and Computer Theory, Cryptography and Image Processing, Petri-Nets Modelling, Multi-Objective Searching Problems (Tabu Search, Particle Swarm Optimization, Ants Colony Optimization, Artificial Life), Parallel Solving Problems (Multi-Agent Systems, Natural Computing, Evolutive Computing and Genetic Algorithms, Local and Global Search), Automatic Planning, Algorithms Analysis.
